Our Mission

To deliver modern, durable, and sustainable cladding solutions that transform infrastructure across Zambia. We aim to combine design innovation with practical functionality for long-lasting impact.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of cladding solutions in the region, recognized for quality, reliability, and eco-conscious practices that support community growth and development.

Our Values

  • Integrity — honest and transparent in all our dealings.
  • Innovation — embracing modern design and technology.
  • Sustainability — eco-friendly practices for future generations.
  • Excellence — delivering projects that exceed expectations.

Our Journey

2012 - Company Founded

Company was operating under the name INCH Modern Technology

2019 - Rebranded

due to expansion into science laboratory construction and installations INCH was rebranded to Cladding Investments

2020 - Service Expansion

Expanded services to include electrical, plumbing, and kitchen modernization projects and general supplies of lab kitchen,computer,mining machines spares and equipment.

2021 - Industry Recognition

Awarded for excellence in educational facility construction and sustainable building practices.

2025 - Future Growth

Continuing to expand our services and impact across Zambia's construction industry.

Meet Our CEO

Mr. Innocent Chanda

Chief Executive Officer

With over 15 years of experience in the construction and cladding industry, Mr. Innocent Chanda leads Cladding Investments with a vision for innovation and excellence. His expertise in modern construction techniques and commitment to sustainable development has positioned the company as a leader in transforming educational and commercial infrastructure across Zambia.

Under his leadership, Cladding Investments has successfully completed numerous projects, including state-of-the-art science laboratories, computer labs, and modernized kitchen facilities. His dedication to quality craftsmanship and client satisfaction continues to drive the company's growth and reputation.
